French Toast

Description

A simple egg custard is used to coat your favourite bread before being fried and covered in sugar. Served with a handful of berries and a dollop of Greek yogurt for the perfect brunch

    Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp whole milk
  • 3 tbsp double cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 thick slices of sourdough
  • Â½ tsp mixed spice
  • 25g caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

    Steps

  1. Lightly beat the eggs in a shallow bowl with the milk, cream and vanilla. Dip the bread in the bowl, allowing to soak for 1-2 mins on each side. You may want to do this in batches.
  2. Mix the mixed spice into the sugar on a large plate.
  3. Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan and cook the bread for 2 mins on each side. Press the bread carefully into the sugar on each side before transferring to a plate. Serve with Greek yogurt and berries, if you like.
